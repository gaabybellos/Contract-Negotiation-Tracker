Thank you for your interest in Negotiation Tracker! This document explains how you can participate in the project.

## 🤝 Current Contribution Policy

**Pull requests are not being accepted at this time.**

This decision is intentional to:
- Maintain consolidated copyright ownership
- Preserve the option for dual licensing in the future (e.g., offering commercial licenses)
- Ensure a clear and unambiguous legal foundation for the project

### What This Means

- ✅ **Forks are welcome** - You're free to fork and modify under AGPL-3.0 terms
- ✅ **Commercial use under AGPL is allowed** - Use it for your business, just comply with AGPL
- ❌ **PRs will not be merged** - Direct code contributions are not accepted currently

## 💬 How You Can Help

Even though we're not accepting PRs, your input is valuable! Here's how you can contribute:

### 1. Report Bugs
Open an issue with:
- A clear, descriptive title
- Steps to reproduce the problem
- Expected vs. actual behavior
- Screenshots if applicable
- Your environment (OS, browser, Node version)

### 2. Suggest Features
Open an issue with:
- A clear description of the feature
- The problem it solves
- Any implementation ideas you have

### 3. Improve Documentation
Found a typo or unclear documentation? Open an issue describing what needs to be improved.

### 4. Share Feedback
General feedback about usability, design, or functionality is always welcome in the discussions or issues.
