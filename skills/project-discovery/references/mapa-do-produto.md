# Product Map Reference

Contract Negotiation Tracker is organized around these initial QA domains:

- contracts: creation, selection, duplication, completion, archiving, deletion;
- clauses: creation, editing, viewing, deletion, metadata, 3-text content;
- comparison: diff pairs, inline view, side-by-side view, statistics;
- filters: search, status, priority, owner, impact category, risk level, sorting;
- versions: snapshots, rounds, party attribution, restore;
- templates: reusable clauses, import, duplicate, template-based contracts;
- import-export: CSV, JSON, DOCX/PDF/TXT parsing where implemented;
- timeline: negotiation events and history;
- playbook: topics, positions, objections, related clause guidance;
- settings: owners, columns, impact categories, theme and preferences.

Before writing module artifacts, check `Docs/qa/module-map.md`.
